TOP DESTINATION

Luzon

RICE TERRACES

Ifugao, Philippines

Heritage site that consists of 2,000 year old terraces and referred as the "Eight Wonders of Earth" Today, the Rice Terraces are a popular tourist attraction and a symbol of Philippine culture and heritage.

HUNDRED ISLAND

Alaminos, Pangasinan

Beautiful tourist destination that is composed of 124 small islands and islets. Famous activities can be done there like island hopping, snorkeling and diving , adventure activities, island camping and scenic view.

VIGAN

Vigan City

It is a historic city known for its well-preserved Spanish colonial architecture.Earned the title of a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It has numerous historic landmarks that showcase its rich cultural heritage and unique culinary offerings.

BURNHAM PARK

Baguio City

It is a picturesque and serene tourist destination that offers a peaceful respite amidst the city's urban landscape. Visitors can take leisurely boat rides or simply sit by the water's edge and enjoy the scenery.

Visayas

CAMBUGAHAY FALLS

Siquijor Island

It is known for its crystal clear and refreshing turquoise-colored water, surrounded by lush green vegetation and rock formations.The hike to the falls is an adventure in itself, leading to the falls and island's natural beauty.

CUATRO ISLAS

Leyte

It is a Paradise Island composed of 4 islands with their own unique charm and beauty. It boast of pristine beaches with fine white sand, crystal clear turquoise waters, and diverse marine life perfect destination scuba diving.

BORACAY

Aklan Province

A world-renowned tropical island destination known for its white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant nightlife. It also has accommodations, restaurants, and bars to suit every budget and preference.

CHOCOLATE HILLS

Bohol Province

It is a unique geological formation composed of over a thousand conical-shaped hills that are spread over an area. It is home to diverse flora and fauna, including endemic species such as the tarsier, one of the world's smallest primates.

Mindanao

EDEN NATURE PARK

Davao City

It offers a wide range of activities such as nature trekking, fishing, swimming etch. You can also experience the SkyRider, a zip-line, butterfly sanctuary, a vegetable garden, and an animal sanctuary where visitors can interact.

WHITE ISLAND

Mambajao, Camiguin

It is a paradise for beachgoers and nature enthusiasts. It offers a serene atmosphere, where visitors can relax and unwind with beautiful scenery. The crystal-clear waters surrounding the sandbar are perfect for swimming and snorkeling.

MOUNT APO

Davao City

It is the highest mountain peak in the Philippines. It is a popular destination for adventure seekers and nature lovers . It is home for range of flora and fauna, including rare and endangered species such as the Philippine eagle.

LAKE SEBU

South Cotabato

It is a natural lake surrounded by rolling hills and lush forests. It is also a popular spot for adventure activities, such as ziplining and kayaking. Also known for its tilapia farming, and visitors can enjoy fresh and delicious tilapia dishes .
